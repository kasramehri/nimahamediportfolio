Sabzeh Meydoon was one of my initial branding projects with the Bongah Brand team. The main challenge was meeting a tight deadline of approximately one month. By providing various benchmarks and utilizing Marty Neumeier’s methodology, we developed a comprehensive brand strategy. Following approval, we also selected the brand name. One of the project’s challenges was to create a brand for fruits and vegetables that would appeal to the general public, balancing a fresh produce offering with a vibrant, popular appearance.
