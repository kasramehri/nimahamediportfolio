I recently had the opportunity to design a lighting project for a competition. The theme of the competition was "Illuminating the Future," and I was tasked with creating a lighting installation that incorporated innovative and sustainable technologies. For my project, I decided to focus on the concept of "smart lighting." I wanted to create a system that was not only visually appealing, but also energy efficient and responsive to the needs of the space it was installed in.
